❤️Our Story❤️

When we were children, the best chips we ever ate were made at home. We grew Nendran banana plants on our own land, harvested them at the right time, and watched our mother turn them into thin, crispy chips with care and patience.

The coconut oil she used was just as pure — coconuts harvested from the same land, dried naturally under the sun, and cold-pressed. No preservatives. No shortcuts. Just real food, made the way it always was.

So thin and crisp they would break at a touch — that is the taste we grew up with. And that is the same way we make Vaazhaii today.

We don't just make chips.
We make them the same way we eat at home — and share them with you.
